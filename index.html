About Us

Welcome to The English Park – where learning meets guidance, and every student is empowered to succeed.


Founded in 2008 by Mr. Daljeet Singh, The English Park has been a trusted name in academic coaching for students from Class 7 to 10. With over a decade of dedicated teaching experience, Mr. Singh has built a nurturing environment that focuses on clarity, confidence, and consistent growth.


At The English Park, we believe in quality over quantity. That’s why we keep our batches small, ensuring each student gets personalized attention and support. Our approach blends strong fundamentals with practical application through regular doubt-clearing sessions, mock tests, and individual progress tracking.


We offer both online and offline classes, giving students the flexibility to learn in the way that suits them best. Whether you're brushing up on grammar, mastering writing skills, or building a strong base for academic excellence, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.


Join The English Park—where your success is our mission.
